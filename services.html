Custom Club Fitting

No two swings are identical. Our certified fitting specialists use Trackman 4 and FlightScope launch monitor to annually analyse your swing data and match you precisely to the shaft flex, clubbed design, loft , lie and grip combination that maximises perfomance for your unique swing.

We fit all major brands including TaylorMade, Callaway , Titleist, PING , Mizuno, Srixon and Cleveland.

  • Full bag or single club fitting sessions
  • Trackman 3 & FlightScope Mevo+ technology
  • Certified fitters for all major OEM brands
  • Written swing data report provided
  • Post purchase follow up session included
