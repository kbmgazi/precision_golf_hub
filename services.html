Service 1

Custom Club Fitting

No two swings are identical. Our certified fitting specialists use Trackman 4 and FlightScope launch monitor to annually analyse your swing data and match you precisely to the shaft flex, clubbed design, loft , lie and grip combination that maximises perfomance for your unique swing.

We fit all major brands including TaylorMade, Callaway , Titleist, PING , Mizuno, Srixon and Cleveland.

  • Full bag or single club fitting sessions
  • Trackman 3 & FlightScope Mevo+ technology
  • Certified fitters for all major OEM brands
  • Written swing data report provided
  • Post purchase follow up session included

Service 02

Golf Ball Personalisation

Add your name , initials, club ,company logo or any custom text and artwork to premium golf balls. Perfect for identifying your ball on the course, corporate gifting , golf days and tournament prizes.

We personalise all Titleist Pro V1 , TaylorMade TP5, Callaway chrome soft and Srixion Z-star models with a fast 5-7 business day turnaround on orders.

  • Text, numbers, logo and artwork printing
  • Premium ball brands only (Pro V1 , TP5 , Chrome Soft)
  • Minimum order: 1 dozen per design
  • Bulk corporate discounts available
  • 5-7 business day turnaround standard; rush available

Service 03

Equipment Consulting

Confused about what's in your bag? Our equipment consultants provide completely unbaised , data backed recommendations that match your swing , goals and budget with zero sales pressure. We work for your game, not for any brand.

Whether you're building your first set, upgrading after years with the same clubs, or second guessing a recent purchase, our consultants provide clarity backed by real launch monitor data.

  • 60 minute one on one consultation session
  • Swing assessment on the range included
  • Brand agnostic recommendations
  • Budget pplanning and prioritisation advice
  • Written equipment report delivered after session

Service 04

Club Repair & Maintainance

Our fully equipped in house workshop keeps your clubs performing like new. From routine re-gripping to full shaft replacements loft/lie adjustments, our OEM-certifed technicians handle it all with precision and speed.

Same day service is available on most common repairs and we carry over 60 grip models from Golf Pride , Lamkin, SuperStroke and Winn.

  • Re-gripping - 60+ grip brands and models in stock
  • Graphite & lie angle adjustment (bending service)
  • Ferrule replacement and cosmetic restoration
  • Same day service available on most repairs
