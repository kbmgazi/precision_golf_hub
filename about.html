About Us

Learn about our history, mission and the partners who helps us deliver world-class golf services.

Our History

Precision Golf was founded in 2024 by Danie Louw, a former PGA professional golfer. Danie opened a small fitting studio in Sandton with one simple belief: the right equipment, properly fitted can transform any golfer's game.

Word spreads quickly. By 2011 the studio had grown into a full service golf centre with a retail floor, a coaching academy and repair workshop.

Today we have three locations across South Africa - Johannesburg, Durban and Cape Town. We help thousands of golfers every year.

Our Mission

Our Mission is simple: to give every golfer- no matter their skill level or budget - access to expert fitting, professional coaching and quality equipment.

We are guided by three values:

  • Precision - We pay attention to the small details because in golf they matter.
  • Integrity - We always recommend what is best for you not what earns us the most.
  • Growth - We believe every golfer can improve and it is our job to help them do so.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted golf destination a place where handicaps fall, champions are made and every golfer leaves better than they arrived.
