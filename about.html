Our Story

From One Studio to South Africa's Golf Hub

Precison Golf Hub was founded in 2008 by a former PGA professional Danie Louw with a single fitting studio in Sandton . His belief was simple: the right equipment , properly fitted , can transform any golfer's experience of the game.

Word spread.Withing three years, the studio expanded into a full service centre encompassing retail, coaching and repair. Today , with flagship location Johannesburg, Cape Town and Durban ,Precision Golf Hub serves thousands of golfers annually.
